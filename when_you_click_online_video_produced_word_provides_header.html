Reflecting on the current global situation, it is clear that education must adapt and change can be difficult.
The goal of this initiative is to support educators in providing digital learning through hands-on microlearning.
The content is focused on implementing specific use cases, in a detailed step by step approach.

educator.support is a non-profit initiative.

Educators play a critical role in shaping the young generations and they need support.
The change from clasroom towards digital education brings new challenges.
At the same time, is also uncovers new opportunities for elevating the learning outcome.

Storytelling - using a framework, validate the story currently being told and help to enhance it
Content - assess existing materials and provide design-related recommendations
Templates - assist with the creation of reusable content and structure
Technology - address technical questions/difficulties related to the learning tools that are used
Practice - have a planned mock rehearsal and prepared for delivering content
Feedback - facilitate feedback gathering regarding the learning experience
